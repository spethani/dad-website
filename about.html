NURUDDIN PETHANI

Personal and Caring

Nuruddin Pethani, CPA is a people’s accountant. He guides you through the confusing maze of taxes with the ease of an expert professional. Always accessible he believes in saving your taxes and treats every tax return as if it was his own. He achieves great results through innovative and legal tax planning ideas. He is an accountant you can depend on and he holds your hand every step of the way.

Competent

Nuruddin Pethani joined the firm in the Year 2000 and went up the hierarchy to become a Partner. His fields of expertise are the Healthcare, Hospitality, Technology businesses. He also handles small to medium-sized audits. Retirement, Estate and Tax Planning for Corporation, LLC’s, and Individual clients are his forte.

Nuruddin believes that no client is small and it is the accountant’s responsibility to help a professional or entrepreneur reach his full potential. His clients range from start-up businesses to multi-million dollar corporations. He reviews each tax return. The clients trust and value his advice completely in tax and financial matters.

Qualified

Nuruddin Pethani besides being a Certified Public Accountant is also a Chartered Accountant from India. He has completed a thorough and grueling 3-year articleship process to master his accounting and tax skills before coming to the US. He is a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants.
